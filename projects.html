🧃 Boba Tracker

My new year's resolution for 2021 was to drink less boba, since I had spent so much money on it in 2020. And also it's not good for my health.

Therefore, I decided to make this webapp to track each time I drink it, and show statistics like how much money I've spent on it and when I last had it in order to discourage myself from getting boba too much.

I mainly made this webapp in order to learn new javascript and front-end development frameworks.

For this project, I learned how to use Pug and Svelte. I also learned how to connect a basic database such as Google Sheets to the front end UI. The spreadsheet holds all the information about the different drinks that I consume throughout the year.

💉 NY COVID Data

During Fall 2020, New York covid guidelines required that travelers from "hotspot" states had to quarantine for 2 weeks. However, these "hotspot" states changed every week, and the criteria for being a "hotspot" state was confusing.

I took data from the COVID Tracking Project and was able to make a table that showed which states were "hotspot" states, and which ones were close to being so. This helped students in New York make the decision about whether or not it was worth to go home if they had to quarantine when they came back.
